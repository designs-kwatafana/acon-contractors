Renovation

At Acon Contractors we take pride in transforming spaces and bringing our clients' visions to life. With a focus on quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction, we offer a wide range of renovation services tailored to meet the unique needs of each project. Our Renovation Services:

1. Interior Renovations:
  • Kitchen remodeling, including cabinetry, countertops, and appliances
  • Bathroom renovations, from fixtures and tiling to plumbing and lighting
  • Basement finishing and remodeling
  • Flooring installation and refinishing
  • Custom carpentry and millwork
  • Painting and wallpapering
2. Exterior Renovations:
  • Deck construction and restoration
  • Siding replacement and repair
  • Roofing repairs and replacements
  • Window and door installation
  • Outdoor living space design and construction
  • Landscaping and hardscaping
3. Whole Home Renovations:
  • Comprehensive home remodeling projects that encompass multiple areas of the home
  • Structural modifications and additions
  • Space planning and design
  • Energy-efficient upgrades
  • Aging-in-place modifications
4. Commercial Renovations:
  • Office renovations and fit-outs
  • Retail space remodeling
  • Restaurant and hospitality renovations
  • Tenant improvements
  • Building facelifts and upgrades
  • Compliance with commercial building codes and regulations

Why Choose Us:
  • Skilled Craftsmanship: Our team of skilled tradespeople and craftsmen are experts in their respective fields, ensuring top-quality workmanship on every project.
  • Personalized Approach: We work closely with our clients to understand their vision, preferences, and budget, tailoring our services to meet their specific needs.
  • Project Management: From planning and design to execution and completion, we oversee every aspect of the renovation process to ensure a seamless and successful outcome.
  • Timely Completion: We prioritize efficiency and strive to complete projects on time and within budget, without compromising on quality.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our commitment to customer satisfaction drives us to go above and beyond to deliver results that exceed expectations and create spaces that inspire.

Whether you are looking to update a single room or transform your entire property, Acon Contractors is equipped to handle all your renovation needs. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover how we can help you achieve your renovation goals.
