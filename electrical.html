Electrical

Acon Contractors is committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient solutions to meet the diverse needs of our community. With a team of highly skilled and licensed electricians, we offer a comprehensive range of electrical services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

1. Residential Electrical Services:
  • Installation and repair of electrical panels and circuit breakers
  • Wiring and rewiring of homes
  • Installation and repair of lighting fixtures, outlets, and switches
  • Ceiling fan and light fixture installation
  • Electrical safety inspections and upgrades
  • Surge protection and lightning arrestor installation
2. Commercial Electrical Services:
  • Installation and maintenance of electrical systems for commercial buildings
  • Lighting design and installation for offices, retail spaces, and restaurants
  • Electrical system upgrades and retrofits
  • Emergency lighting and exit sign installation
  • Electrical safety inspections and code compliance
  • Electrical project management for new construction and renovations
3. Industrial Electrical Services:
  • Installation and maintenance of electrical systems for industrial facilities
  • Motor control and automation system installation and repair
  • Electrical system design and engineering
  • Preventive maintenance and predictive maintenance programs
  • Power quality analysis and solutions
  • Electrical safety training and consulting
4. Emergency Electrical Services:
  • 24/7 emergency response for urgent electrical issues
  • Electrical system troubleshooting and repair
  • Temporary power solutions for construction sites and events
  • Electrical fire and damage repair
  • Rapid response and efficient resolution of emergency situations
5. Energy-Efficient Solutions:
  • Installation of energy-efficient lighting systems, such as LED and solar-powered lighting
  • Electrical system upgrades to improve energy efficiency
  • Energy audits and recommendations for reducing energy consumption
  • Installation of electric vehicle charging stations
  • Renewable energy system installation, including solar panels and wind turbines

Why Choose Us:
  • Licensed and Insured Electricians: Our team of electricians are licensed, insured, and trained to the highest industry standards, ensuring the safety and quality of our work.
  • Commitment to Safety: Safety is our top priority, and we adhere to strict safety protocols to protect our clients, our team, and the community.
  • Responsive Service: We understand the importance of prompt and efficient service, and we strive to respond to our clients' needs quickly and effectively.
  • Competitive Pricing: We offer competitive and transparent pricing, ensuring that our clients receive the best value for their money.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our commitment to customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions that exceed their expectations.

Whether you need routine maintenance, emergency repairs, or a complete electrical system overhaul, Acon contractors is here to assist you. Contact us today to schedule an appointment or to learn more about how we can help with your electrical needs.
